AutoClicker: Record & Replay lets you click anywhere on a webpage, save those positions as a numbered sequence, and replay them automatically — on demand or on a loop.

No scripts. No code. Just click, record, play.

─────────────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
─────────────────────────────────────

1. RECORD
Open the extension popup and press Start Recording. A subtle outline appears around the page and your cursor turns into a crosshair. Click every spot on the page you want to automate — a numbered purple marker appears at each position. When you're done, hit the Stop Recording button in the corner of the page.

2. CONFIGURE
Reopen the popup to see your full click sequence. Set the delay between each click in seconds, and toggle Loop if you want the sequence to repeat forever.

3. PLAY
Hit Play. The extension clicks each recorded position in order, highlighting the active step in orange as it goes. Hit Stop at any time.

─────────────────────────────────────
FEATURES
─────────────────────────────────────

✦ Visual click recording — see exactly where each click will land with numbered on-screen markers
✦ Precise coordinates — clicks are dispatched to the exact pixel you recorded
✦ Ordered playback — steps fire in the sequence you set, every time
✦ Custom delay — set any delay between clicks in seconds (e.g. 0.5, 1, 2.5)
✦ Loop mode — repeat the full sequence continuously until you stop it
✦ Reorder steps — move any step up or down in the sequence with one click
✦ Delete steps — remove individual steps or clear everything and start fresh
✦ Persistent memory — your recorded steps are saved automatically and survive page reloads and browser restarts
✦ Works on any website — no configuration needed per site

─────────────────────────────────────
GREAT FOR
─────────────────────────────────────

• Online surveys and repetitive form submissions
• Multi-step web workflows you run regularly
• QA testing and UI automation without writing code
• Any task where you click the same spots over and over

─────────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY
─────────────────────────────────────

AutoClicker: Record & Replay collects zero personal data. Your recorded click sequences are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage — they are never sent to any server, never shared with anyone, and can be deleted instantly using the Clear All Steps button.

Permissions used:
• activeTab — to interact with the current tab when the popup is open
• scripting — to inject the recording and playback script into the page
• storage — to save your click sequence locally between sessions
