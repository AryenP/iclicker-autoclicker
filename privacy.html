Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

AutoClicker: Record & Replay is a Chrome browser extension for recording and replaying click sequences on webpages. This policy explains how the extension handles data.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Local Storage

The extension stores your recorded click sequences (screen coordinates and element labels) locally using Chrome's storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server or third party
  • Can be cleared at any time using the "Clear All Steps" button in the popup

Permissions

  • activeTab — to interact with the current browser tab when the extension icon is clicked
  • scripting — to inject the click-recording and playback script into the active page
  • storage — to save your recorded click sequence locally between browser sessions

No permission is used to access browsing history, passwords, cookies, or any personal information.

Third Parties

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs of any kind.

Changes

Any future changes to this policy will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
